Features

More than a typing counter

Isometric HHKB Farm

Each key is a plot of land. Watch your farm evolve from barren soil to a lush garden as you type throughout the day.

Heatmap View

Toggle heatmap mode to visualize your typing patterns. Height and color reflect the total presses on each key.

100 Species to Collect

Discover crops across 4 rarity tiers — Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Legendary. Track your collection progress.

Farm Animals

As your farm grows, ducks will show up to help fertilize and harvest crops. A loyal dog patrols around — try moving your mouse and see what happens.

Hire Workers

Unlock up to 5 workers who auto-harvest your crops. The more you farm, the more helpers you can recruit.

🔍

Zoom & Resize

Scroll to zoom in and out of your farm. Drag the window edge to resize. Your farm looks great at any scale.

Tip: You can scroll to zoom in/out and resize the window freely — your farm adapts to any size!
